Chicken Burger Recipes

Picture Size: 310x310px

Description

Grilled Chicken Burger is a juicy sandwich made with a seasoned, grilled chicken breast served on a bun with fresh toppings and sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 burger bun

  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Ketchup and Mayo

Recipe

  1. Season the chicken breast with oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika..
  2. Grill it for 4 to 6 minutes per side until cooked through.
  3. Toast the burger bun lightly.
  4. Add sauce, lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, and the grilled chicken to the bun.
