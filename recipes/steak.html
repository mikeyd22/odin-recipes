Steak Recipes

Picture Size: 310x310px

Description

Steak is a cut of meat known for its rich flavor and tenderness, often grilled or pan-seared for a delicious, hearty meal

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Gralic (optional)
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme

Recipe

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper (or your favorite seasoning).
  2. Preheat a pan or grill to high heat.
  3. Cook steak 3 to 5 minutes per side (depending on thickness and preferred doneness).
  4. Rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
